I am now a postdoc working at the National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology, Japan (AIST, 産業技術総合研究所) in the Microbial and Genetic Resources Research Group. My current research topic is revealing the evolutionary hostory of microorganisms occurring in natural and engieered ecosystems with Dr. Masaru K. Nobu. I got my Master’s and PhD degree both in Environmental Science and Engingeering from the University of Illinos at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), where most of my research focused on microbial ecology in wastewater treatment processes. I received my Bachelor’s of Engineering degree from Peking University (北京大学), China.
